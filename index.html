WOMEN IN THE MUSLIM WORLD
Islamic nations around the world have strict rules and regulations for women. These rules are essentially embedded in their political systems, resulting in gender inequality for women in the Muslim world. It is important to note that these nations have significantly high rates of poverty, indicating a trend between gender inequality and high poverty rates.
Female empowerment is a major step toward alleviating poverty. Not only will it bring in more economic flow, but it will also help create a better society. Women in the Muslim world are often suppressed by patriarchal societies. Female empowerment and gender equality will benefit not only families but also whole economies.
